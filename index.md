# E-Matrix Tasks

Essential matrix for productivity.

E-Matrix Tasks is a simple macOS app that helps you manage your daily tasks using the Eisenhower Matrix.  
Focus on what really matters and stop drowning in endless to-do lists.

---

## Why E-Matrix Tasks?

- **Eisenhower Matrix**: organise tasks by urgency and importance.
- **Clear daily focus**: always see what you must do *now* and what can wait.
- **Minimalist interface**: no distractions, only your tasks.
- **macOS native**: built with SwiftUI, feels right at home on your Mac.

---

## Key features

- Four quadrants:  
  - Important & Urgent  
  - Important & Not Urgent  
  - Not Important & Urgent  
  - Not Important & Not Urgent
- Due dates and statuses for tasks
- Filters and views for your daily planning
- Light, calm design to reduce stress
