## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**1. How do I create a new task?**  
Click the “+” button in the quadrant where you want to add a task, then fill in the title and (optionally) the due date.

**2. Can I move a task to another quadrant?**  
Yes. Open the task and change its importance/urgency settings — it will be moved automatically.

**3. How do I mark a task as done?**  
Open the task and change its status to “Done”, or use the quick action (if available in the app).
